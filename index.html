About Me

Background

Hi, my name is Griselda Pasillas, I am a recent graduate with a Master’s in Cyber Forensics and Security from Illinois Institute of Technology, complementing my Bachelor’s in Information Technology and Management. My coursework has enabled me to enhance my understanding in Cyber Warfare, Coding Security, Digital Evidence, Database Security, IT Auditing, Cyber Forensics, Governance Risk & Compliance, as well as Legal Ethical Issues in Information Technology.

I have acquired skills in several technologies including Windows OS, MacOS, Figma, GitHub, Python, HTML, Java, and cybersecurity tools such as Wireshark, Azure, and Checkpoint. In my previous position as a Junior System Administrator at Informa Connect Foodservice, I gained experience in system optimization, security assessment, and risk mitigation using Azure, to improve system performance and security protocols.

Career Aspirations

Beyond my technical skills, I am dedicated to strengthening digital security and improving system performance. I aspire to apply my knowledge in cyber forensics and security to help resolve challenges in changing environments and to communicate with professionals to collaborate on the implementation of defense and compliance standards.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new places, diving into culinary adventures to learn about different cultures, and being active through strength training. These experiences encourage my creativity to grow and fuel my resilience, allowing me to apply my problem-solving skills and discipline in cybersecurity.
